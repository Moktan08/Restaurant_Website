Chef's Quote

I tell a student that the most important class you can take is technique. A great chef is first a great technician. 'If you are a jeweler, or a surgeon or a cook, you have to know the trade in your hand. You have to learn the process. You learn it through endless repetition until it belongs to you. And when it finally belongs to you, finding the happiness and finding the satisfaction and finding fulfillment in watching people eat your food with happiness in their face, is what makes you a true chef.
